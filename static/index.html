Climate Data Explorer

Local summaries of global climate models.

Climate science research generally happens at the global scale but the questions we face around climate change can involve more local decision making. This site allows you to compare monthly temperature and precipitation data between measured current conditions, modeled past climates and predicted future climates for any point on earth. The shaded areas on the chart represents the range of modeled climate values while the lines represent the median result across all available models for that time period.

Data analysis, web services, and interface development by Matthew T. Perry
This site is powered by rasterstats, Mapbox.js, D3.js, and Flask
Data source: 10 arc-minute resolution data from www.worldclim.org.

Hijmans, R.J., S.E. Cameron, J.L. Parra, P.G. Jones and A. Jarvis, 2005. Very high resolution interpolated climate surfaces for global land areas. International Journal of Climatology 25: 1965-1978.
