Climate Data Explorer

Local summaries of global climate models

Climate science research generally happens at the global scale but the questions we face around climate change can involve more local decision making. We need to understand a location's unique past, present and future climates in order to prepare for potential local impacts from global climate change. This site allows you to visualize monthly temperature and precipitation data and compare the results between measured current conditions, modeled past climates and predicted future climates for any terrestrial point location on earth. The shaded areas on the chart represents the range of modeled climate results while the lines represent the median value across all available models for that time period.
